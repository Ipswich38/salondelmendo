About Salon delMENdo

We believe beauty knows no boundaries. Our salon is a modern sanctuary where men and women come together to experience exceptional service in an inclusive, welcoming environment.

Since opening our doors on October 28, 2022, we've been dedicated to providing premium beauty and wellness services. In our years of service, our team of skilled professionals has stayed at the forefront of beauty trends while maintaining the classic techniques that never go out of style.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll experience personalized service tailored to your unique style and preferences. We're not just about looking good – we're about feeling confident and radiant.
